Supply List

Essentials (Bring to every class)

  • Pencils: B, 2B, 4B
  • Eraser-pen thing (Factis or Tombow brand)
  • Gummy eraser
  • 9"x11" Sketchbook (at least 89 gsm paper)
  • Blending stumps

Optional

  • A good watercolor set
  • Some watercolor brushes (synthetic bristles) (don't overpay for sable brushes)
  • A good gouache set (Holbein acrylic polymer gouache is good)
  • A small pad of Arches watercolor paper
  • Frog Tape (for hard edges)
  • Cork-backed Ruler
  • X-acto Knife (with safety cap)
  • A Portfolio Bag (don't get an enormous one)
